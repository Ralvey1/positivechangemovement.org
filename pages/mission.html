Our Mission

Positive Change Movement is dedicated to empowering individuals living with HIV through advocacy, education, and support. Our mission is to dismantle the stigma and barriers surrounding HIV, while ensuring access to healthcare and services for all affected individuals.

Our Vision

We envision a world where people living with HIV are free from discrimination, fully supported in their healthcare, and empowered to lead fulfilling lives. We aim to foster communities that embrace health equity, respect, and inclusivity.
