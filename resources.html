Community Resources

Empowering individuals and building healthier, stronger communities.

Support for Our Communities

Positive Change Movement provides essential tools, education, and networks to empower individuals and families affected by HIV/AIDS. Our resources are built to foster resilience, access to care, and community leadership.

Available Resources

Medical Support

Access to trusted healthcare providers specializing in HIV treatment and holistic care navigation.

Education Resources

Community workshops, webinars, and printed materials on HIV prevention, U=U, and legal rights.

Peer Support

Peer-led groups and one-on-one navigation programs offering empowerment through shared experience.

Financial Assistance

Connections to programs offering financial aid and assistance for medical needs, housing, and transportation.

Community Events

Engage with local and statewide events promoting awareness, support, and advocacy for PLWHA.
