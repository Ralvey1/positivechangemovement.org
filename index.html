About Positive Change Movement

Positive Change Movement exists to dismantle systemic racism, serophobia, and performative activism embedded in HIV service organizations. We shift power back to the people living with HIV, demanding accountability and ensuring real action over empty promises.

What CHANGE Means to Us

Championing Health

Bridging gaps in care through peer support, navigation, and community health worker programs.

Health Equity

Ensuring access to equitable care and addressing systemic health disparities.

Advocacy

Fighting for HIV decriminalization, anti-oppressive policies, and human rights protections.

Nurturing Growth

Providing education, leadership opportunities, and empowerment for PLWHA and marginalized communities.

Generating Support

Creating peer-led spaces for support, understanding, and resource access.

Ending Stigma

Promoting education and awareness campaigns like U=U (Undetectable = Untransmittable).
