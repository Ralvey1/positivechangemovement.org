Empowering People Living with HIV

We are a peer-led community, working together to provide support, advocacy, and education for people living with HIV. Together, we challenge stigma and create lasting change.

Our Mission

Positive Change Movement is a community-driven initiative Championing Health, Advocacy, and Nurturing Growth and Empowerment (CHANGE) for People Living with HIV (PLHIV). We aim to dismantle racism, homophobia, and transphobia to help end the HIV epidemic by promoting equity, understanding, and inclusiveness. Through peer support, decriminalization efforts, and stigma reduction, we empower PLHIV and work to dismantle systemic discrimination and inequality. Our programs are guided by lived experiences, ensuring that those most affected have a voice in shaping the future.

Our Vision

We envision a world where individuals from all backgrounds and identities thrive without facing discrimination, prejudice, or barriers. Positive Change Movement is dedicated to CHANGE—Championing Health, Advocacy, and Nurturing Growth and Empowerment—while actively dismantling oppressive systems. Our goal is to create a future where everyone, regardless of race, sexual orientation, gender identity, or health status, is empowered to live stigma-free, full, and healthy lives in a just and equitable society.

Holistic Health

Our health programs are designed by and for people living with HIV, offering access to medical care, mental health services, and emotional support that meets the needs of our diverse community.

Peer Support

We provide peer-led support groups, housing assistance, and counseling to help individuals navigate life with HIV, ensuring no one goes through it alone.

Advocacy

Our community advocates for policies that protect the rights of people living with HIV, pushing for decriminalization and equitable access to healthcare and resources.

Education

We offer educational programs that provide individuals with the knowledge and tools to manage their health and fight stigma, fostering understanding within and beyond our community.
