Empowering the HIV+ Community

Through advocacy, education, and support.

Health

Positive Change Movement focuses on the health and well-being of those living with HIV, offering access to essential medical services, treatment, and emotional care.

Support

We offer consistent support through peer groups, counseling, and housing assistance for individuals navigating life with HIV.

Advocacy

Positive Change Movement advocates for policy reform to decriminalize HIV and ensure equitable access to healthcare and resources for all PLWHIV.

Education

Our team is dedicated to providing the latest information on HIV prevention, treatment options, and reducing stigma through comprehensive education.
