Our Mission

To provide education, advocacy, and support for the HIV+ community, aiming to empower individuals and fight stigma.

Our Services

Support Groups

We host weekly support groups for HIV+ individuals and allies.

Advocacy

Our advocacy work involves pushing for HIV decriminalization and health care reform.

Healthcare Navigation

We assist individuals with navigating healthcare systems and accessing treatment.
