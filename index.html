Our Core Pillars

Holistic Care, By Us, For Us

Peer-led health and emotional support services designed to uplift and empower every individual in our community.

Stronger Together

We provide peer-led support groups, housing assistance, and legal services to ensure that no one is left behind.

Fighting for Our Rights

Our community actively advocates for policy changes to decriminalize HIV and improve access to healthcare.

Lifting Knowledge, Reducing Stigma

We educate our community on HIV treatment, prevention, and fight stigma through shared experiences and real stories.
