Our Mission

Positive Change Movement is a community-driven initiative championing health, advocacy, and nurturing growth and empowerment (CHANGE) for People Living with HIV (PLHIV). We focus on HIV peer support, decriminalization, and ending stigma through advocacy and reform, empowering communities throughout Kentucky and fostering change wherever itâ€™s needed.

Support Groups

We host weekly support groups for HIV+ individuals and allies, offering emotional and social support.

Advocacy

Our advocacy work involves pushing for HIV decriminalization and health care reform through action and legislation.

Healthcare Navigation

We assist individuals with navigating healthcare systems and accessing necessary treatment and resources.
