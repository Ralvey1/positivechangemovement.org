Welcome to Positive Change Movement

\
\

Our Mission

\

Empowering the HIV+ community through advocacy, education, and support. We believe in fostering positive change by providing resources and peer support for people living with HIV/AIDS in Kentucky and beyond.

\
\
\ \
\

Our Vision

\
\

To create a world where individuals living with HIV are free from stigma, fully supported, and empowered to thrive in their communities. Our goal is to foster a society that values inclusivity, equity, and the well-being of all people, regardless of their HIV status.

\
\
\ \
\

About Us

\

Positive Change Movement is a community-based organization dedicated to advocating for the rights of individuals living with HIV. We prioritize MIPA (Meaningful Involvement of People with HIV/AIDS) principles, ensuring that all our programs are peer-driven, providing not just resources but empathy and understanding.

\
\ \
\

Our Services

\
\
\

Community Health Workers

\

Our CHW program connects people living with HIV to peer navigators who can help bridge gaps in healthcare and social services.

\
\
\

Education & Outreach

\

We provide educational workshops and outreach programs to spread awareness about HIV prevention, U=U, and stigma reduction.

\
\
\

Advocacy

\

We advocate for policy changes at local and state levels to ensure that healthcare systems are inclusive and equitable for all people living with HIV.
