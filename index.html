Our Vision and Mission

Positive Change Movement envisions a world where all people living with HIV/AIDS, especially those from marginalized communities, are empowered, supported, and have equal access to resources and care. Our mission is to challenge stigma, advocate for justice, provide education, and build a community-driven support system for those affected by HIV/AIDS.
