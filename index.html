Our mission is to empower, advocate, and support people living with HIV/AIDS while fostering inclusive and compassionate communities.

Our Vision & Mission

Positive Change Movement envisions a world where every individual, including marginalized communities, can thrive free from stigma, inequality, and barriers. Our mission is to provide holistic support, education, and advocacy to empower individuals and create inclusive communities for people living with or affected by HIV/AIDS.
