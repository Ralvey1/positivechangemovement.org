Why Peer Support Matters

Peer support is at the heart of Positive Change Movement’s mission. By connecting people with shared experiences, we foster understanding, reduce stigma, and build a supportive community for People Living with HIV (PLHIV).

Benefits of Peer Support

  • Empathy & Understanding: Peer support connects individuals who can relate to each other’s experiences, providing a unique level of empathy and insight.
  • Reducing Isolation: Being part of a community helps reduce feelings of isolation, offering a space for connection and friendship.
  • Improving Health Outcomes: Studies show that peer support can improve mental health, increase adherence to treatment, and enhance overall well-being.

Our Peer Support Services

Positive Change Movement offers a variety of peer-led support services, including:

  • Empowerment Groups: Regularly scheduled empowerment groups where PLHIV can share experiences, challenges, and victories in a safe, confidential environment.
  • One-on-One Peer Counseling: Individual peer counseling sessions that offer personalized support and guidance from trained peers.
  • Workshops & Events: Educational and social events that focus on health, wellness, and empowerment for our community.

Get Involved

There are many ways to engage with our peer support community:

  • Join a Group: Become part of an empowerment group to connect with others who share your journey.
  • Become a Peer Leader: Use your lived experience to support others by training as a peer leader or counselor.
  • Attend Events: Join us for community events to learn, connect, and celebrate together.

Interested in getting involved? Contact us to learn more about how you can participate in or support our peer-led initiatives.
