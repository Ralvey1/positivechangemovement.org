Advocacy for Positive Change

At Positive Change Movement, we are committed to advocating for the rights of People Living with HIV (PLHIV). We strive to dismantle harmful policies, end stigma, and promote health equity for all.

Our Advocacy Goals

  • Decriminalization: Advocating for the reform of outdated HIV criminalization laws.
  • Equitable Healthcare: Working towards an inclusive healthcare system.
  • Ending Stigma: Promoting understanding through education and outreach.

How You Can Get Involved

  • Contact Your Representatives: Advocate for equitable healthcare policies.
  • Attend Advocacy Events: Join us to raise awareness and share stories.
  • Volunteer: Become an advocate to support those affected by HIV.

If you're ready to take action, contact us for more information on upcoming events and volunteer opportunities.
