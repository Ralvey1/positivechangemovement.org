About Us

Building a Future Led by People Living with HIV.

Who We Are

Positive Change Movement is dedicated to dismantling systemic barriers in HIV care and advocacy. We center the lived experiences of people living with HIV, ensuring that they lead the movement for equity, justice, and real change. We believe that advocacy must move beyond words and into meaningful action, led by those most impacted.

What We Do

Through education, peer-led support programs, policy advocacy, and direct action, Positive Change Movement bridges gaps in care, fights stigma, and empowers individuals to rise as leaders within their communities.

Advocacy at Our Core

We are committed to modernizing outdated HIV laws, dismantling racism and homophobia in healthcare, and promoting a human rights-based approach to health equity. Our advocacy ensures that no one is left behind in the fight for dignity and justice.
