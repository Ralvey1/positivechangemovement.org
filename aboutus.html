About Us

Who We Are

Positive Change Movement is dedicated to supporting individuals affected by HIV/AIDS by providing education, advocacy, and empowerment. We envision a future where everyone has equal access to healthcare, community, and resources to live fulfilling lives.

What We Do

Our mission is to empower and uplift individuals, especially those from marginalized communities, by fostering education, advocacy, and support. Learn about our core values and initiatives below.

Advocacy

We advocate for equitable healthcare policies, human rights, and a stigma-free society.

Education

Providing tools and knowledge to empower individuals and communities about HIV/AIDS.

Support

Creating safe spaces for peer-led support and resource sharing.
