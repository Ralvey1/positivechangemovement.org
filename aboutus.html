Who We Are

Positive Change Movement is a community-led initiative dedicated to empowering People Living with HIV (PLHIV) through advocacy, peer support, education, and reform. Our mission is to challenge stigma, end discrimination, and provide a platform for individuals and communities to thrive.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Positive Change Movement began with a simple idea: to create a safe, supportive space for People Living with HIV (PLHIV) to connect, share their stories, and advocate for their rights. Over time, we have grown into a statewide initiative, offering services that include peer support groups, advocacy training, and holistic health resources for individuals and communities impacted by HIV.

Our Mission

To dismantle racism, homophobia, transphobia, and help end the HIV epidemic by promoting equity, understanding, and inclusiveness. Positive Change Movement aims to create a more just society by addressing systemic discrimination and fostering positive change.

Our Vision

Positive Change Movement envisions a world where individuals from all backgrounds and identities thrive without facing discrimination, prejudice, or barriers. We are dedicated to CHANGE—Championing Health, Advocacy, and Nurturing Growth and Empowerment—while actively dismantling oppressive systems.

  • Race is not a barrier to health and wellness.
  • Substance use is not stigmatized.
  • HIV status does not determine quality of life.
  • HIV transmission is eliminated.

Our goal is to create a future where everyone, regardless of race, sexual orientation, gender identity, or health status, is empowered to live stigma-free, full, and healthy lives in a just and equitable society.

Our Values

  • Equity: We strive to ensure that everyone, regardless of their background, has access to the support and resources they need.
  • Empowerment: We believe in the power of lived experiences and the strength that comes from lifting up our voices to create change.
  • Community: Together, we form a support network that fosters acceptance, understanding, and growth.
  • Inclusion: We honor and respect the diverse identities and experiences within our community.

Our Team

The Positive Change Movement team is made up of passionate individuals, many of whom are People Living with HIV. Each team member brings a unique set of skills and experiences to our organization, united by our commitment to improving the lives of PLHIV through support, advocacy, and education.
