# Positive Change Movement Website

Welcome to the official website for Positive Change Movement (PCM).

Positive Change Movement advances MIPA through statewide Community Health Worker training, certification, leadership development, and advocacy led by people living with HIV (PLHIV). The site now centers PCM's goal of replacing gatekeeping with PLHIV-led decision-making, paid leadership, and accountable HIV service systems.

## Website Overview

This site includes:
- Home page presenting PCM as a MIPA-centered, PLHIV-led statewide CHW leadership program
- About Us page explaining PCM's MIPA foundation and power-shifting mission
- CHW Training page outlining the statewide training, certification, and leadership pathway
- Advocacy page focused on PLHIV-led decision-making, HIV justice, workforce power, and accountability
- Standards page defining expectations for community-rooted, PLHIV-led, accountable HIV service organizations
- Resources for PLHIV and communities in need
- Blog sharing news, stories, and updates
- Contact form for community outreach
- Special campaigns like "Breaking the Silence"
- Join Our Empowerment Network form for volunteers, advocates, and future leaders

## Project Structure

- index.html - Main landing page for visitors
- pages/aboutus.html - Overview of PCM's background, MIPA values, and leadership model
- pages/advocacy.html - Details policy advocacy, HIV justice, CHW workforce power, and accountability priorities
- pages/blog.html - Posts and updates from the community
- breaking-the-silence.html - Campaign page fighting HIV stigma
- pages/contact.html - General contact information
- pages/contact-us.html - Legacy contact form page
- join-our-empowerment-network.html - Volunteer and leadership interest form
- pages/mission.html - Expanded explanation of mission and vision
- pages/peersupport.html - CHW training and certification pathway page
- pages/resources.html - Helpful links and information for PLHIV
- pages/standards.html - PCM standards for PLHIV-led, accountable HIV service organizations
- style.css - Global site styling
- script.js - Responsive navigation and other interactions
- images/ - Website images and graphics
