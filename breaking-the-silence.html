Breaking the Silence

Confronting Stigma Around Identity, Health, and HIV

Our Campaign

"Breaking the Silence" is a Positive Change Movement initiative dedicated to dismantling the stigma that surrounds HIV, identity, and health justice. We believe that silence breeds injustice—and it’s time for change.

Why It Matters

Stigma remains one of the biggest barriers to care, empowerment, and equality for people living with HIV. Our campaign amplifies the voices of those most impacted and fosters communities where speaking truth to power is celebrated, not silenced.

How to Get Involved

  • Share your story or testimonial
  • Join local advocacy and storytelling events
  • Support policy change for HIV decriminalization and health equity
  • Host community dialogues to break down myths and misconceptions

When we speak up, we break the chains of stigma and build the future we deserve. Join Positive Change Movement in Breaking the Silence today.
