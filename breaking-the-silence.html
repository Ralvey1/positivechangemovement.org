Breaking the Silence

Confronting stigma and building PLHIV-led power.

Our Campaign

"Breaking the Silence" is a Positive Change Movement initiative dedicated to dismantling the stigma that surrounds HIV, identity, health, and leadership. Silence protects gatekeeping. Speaking truth builds power.

Why It Matters

Stigma remains one of the biggest barriers to care, employment, leadership, and equal treatment for people living with HIV. This campaign amplifies PLHIV voices while connecting storytelling to MIPA, CHW workforce development, certification, and systems accountability.

How to Get Involved

  • Share your story or testimonial
  • Join local and statewide advocacy events
  • Support HIV decriminalization, U=U education, and health equity
  • Help build the PLHIV-led CHW training and leadership pathway

When we speak up, we break stigma and build the future we deserve. Join Positive Change Movement in replacing gatekeeping with PLHIV leadership.
